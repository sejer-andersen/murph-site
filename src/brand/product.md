# What is Murph

Murph is an AI-first software company in the business of one thing: time.

For most of history, time has been the one asset you couldn't buy. You could hire people, outsource work, or add tools, but you couldn't actually create more hours.

Murph changes that.

We build systems that let companies buy time for the first time, by turning repetitive operations, internal workflows, and expert decision-making into software that runs quietly in the background.

We don't sell generic automation.

We take a consulting-led approach: we work closely with a company to identify where time is being lost, design the right systems, scope the solution, and then build and deploy the infrastructure end-to-end. From diagnosis to execution, Murph owns the entire process.

The result is not just efficiency.

The result is an unfair advantage: a company-specific operating system that compounds over time, reduces dependency on manual labor, and keeps the business ahead as AI reshapes every industry.

Murph exists for organizations that don't want more tools.

They want more time.

Buy time. For the first time.
